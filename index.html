The Studio

Restraint, in rooms built to be lived in — not photographed.

Practice

Full-scope residential interiors, in close collaboration with the architect, from first plan to the final object placed on a shelf.

Approach

Natural materials with weight. Light layered low. Fewer things, better made. A house that ages into itself rather than out of fashion.

Clients

A short, private roster. Most work arrives by introduction. We take on only a handful of homes a year.

Recognition

Architectural Digest · Elle Decor A-List · Veranda · The New York Times. Quietly, over four decades.
