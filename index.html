The Studio

Rooms built to be lived in — not photographed.

Practice

Full-scope residential interiors, in close collaboration with the architect — from first plan to the last object placed.

Approach

Natural materials with weight. Light layered low. Fewer things, better made. A house that ages into itself.

Clientele

A short, private roster. Most work arrives by introduction; we take on only a handful of homes each year.

Recognition

Architectural Digest · Elle Decor A-List · Veranda · The New York Times — quietly, across four decades.
